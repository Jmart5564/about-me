A Quick Introduction

Hello Everyone, Let me introduce myself. My name is Bilbo Baggins the hobbit and today is my eleventy-first birthday! All I really wanted was to live a quiet peaceful life in my hobbit home, tend to my garden and smoke longbottom leaf(The finest in all the Shire). One day, a band of Dwarves showed up at my door(a nasty ol' mess they made) and before I knew it, I was leaving the Shire on a grand adventure. After a year of almost being eaten by trolls, meeting elves, being chased by goblins, riding barrels down a river and eventually breaking into a dragons lair to steal back a mountain of gold, I find myself back at the shire. My most prized possession from that adventure would have to be the mysterious ring I won by out-riddling a quite peculiar creature named gollum. Oh how much I love my ring....my precious....
